**RAMSEUR, NC** – December 9, 2025 – Sentinel Grove Partners, a leading real estate investment and finance firm, announced today that it successfully closed on the debt financing for the sale of a single-tenant Walgreens property located in Ramseur, North Carolina. The investment has concluded with the successful sale of the property and investors have been fully repaid. The transaction represents continued strong investment interest in high-quality, essential retail assets with long-term lease commitments.

The Walgreens store is strategically situated in the Ramseur community, offering essential pharmacy and retail services. The successful closing underscores Sentinel Grove Partners’ expertise in structuring tailored debt solutions for properties anchored by nationally recognized, investment-grade tenants.

Derek Copeland, Principal of Sentinel Grove Partners, commented on the deal: “We are pleased to have successfully executed the debt financing for this Walgreens transaction. This deal highlights the enduring stability and investor demand for well-located, net-leased assets in the essential retail sector. Our ability to provide reliable and efficient capital solutions continues to be a key differentiator for our partners.”

The terms of the sale and the identity of the buyer were not disclosed. Sentinel Grove Partners continues to actively pursue opportunities to finance and invest in stable, income-producing commercial real estate assets across the Southeast and nationally.

**About Sentinel Grove Partners**

Sentinel Grove Partners is a private real estate finance and investment firm focused on providing customized debt and equity solutions for commercial properties. With a focus on strategic assets and strong sponsorship, the firm is committed to delivering exceptional value and fostering long-term relationships with its clients and partners.
